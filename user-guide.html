Complete User Guide & Tutorials for Mutual Fund Analyzer

INTRODUCTION

This Mutual Fund Analyzer allows you to compare any Indian mutual fund with the Nifty50 benchmark index, using: 1-year 3-year 5-year historical data automatically fetched from Yahoo Finance (browser-safe CORS, free, no backend). It provides easy-to-understand statistical performance metrics for informed investment evaluation.

Past performance is not indicative of future results. Returns shown are historical and for illustrative purposes only.

This platform is an analytical and educational tool. It does not constitute investment advice, research recommendation, or solicitation.

Benchmark data is used for comparative analysis only. Risk metrics are derived from historical NAV data and may not reflect future risk.
