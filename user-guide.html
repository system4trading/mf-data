üìä INTERPRETING RESULTS

Below is what each metric means.

‚≠ê Annual Return

How much the fund gained per year on average.

‚≠ê Volatility / SD

Measures how much the NAV fluctuates.

Higher = more risk.

‚≠ê Sharpe Ratio

Risk-adjusted return per unit of total risk

Higher = better

‚≠ê Treynor Ratio

Return per unit of systematic risk (beta)

Useful if fund is well diversified

‚≠ê Beta

How sensitive the fund is to market (Nifty50)

  • >1 ‚Üí more volatile than market

  • <1 ‚Üí less volatile

‚≠ê Alpha

Extra return that fund generated OVER what its beta explains

Positive = outperformer
Negative = underperformer

‚≠ê Correlation

Strength of movement with Nifty50.

‚≠ê R-Squared

How ‚Äúindex-like‚Äù the fund is.

‚≠ê Systematic Risk

Market-related risk.

‚≠ê Unsystematic Risk

Fund-specific risk (diversifiable).

‚≠ê Tracking Error

Deviation vs benchmark‚Äîlower is better for index funds.

‚≠ê CAPM Expected Return

Theoretically expected return given market conditions.
